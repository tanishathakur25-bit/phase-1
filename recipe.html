Sandwitch

ingrediants

  • 2 slices of bread (white, brown, or multigrain)
  • 1-2 tablespoons mayonnaise or butter
  • 1-2 slices of cheese (optional)
  • 1 small tomato, sliced
  • 1 small cucumber, sliced
  • Lettuce leaves or spinach
  • 1-2 slices of cooked chicken, turkey, or ham (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Mustard or ketchup (optional)

Instructions

  • Prepare the bread:
    • Spread butter or mayonnaise on one side of each bread slice.
  • Layer the veggies:
    • Place lettuce, tomato slices, and cucumber on one slice of bread.
  • Add protein and cheese (optional):
    • Place cheese and/or meat slices on top of the veggies.
  • Season:
    • Sprinkle a little salt and pepper, and add mustard or ketchup if you like.
  • Assemble the sandwich:
    • Cover with the other slice of bread, butter/mayo side down.
  • Serve:
    • You can eat it as is or toast it in a sandwich maker for 2-3 minutes until golden and crispy.
